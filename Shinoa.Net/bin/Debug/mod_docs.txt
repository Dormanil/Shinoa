```
Mute a user:
    Usage: [PREFIX]mute @user <[number] seconds/minutes/hours> <"reason" (optional)>
	Example: [PREFIX]mute @OmegaVesko 20 seconds
	Example: [PREFIX]mute @OmegaVesko "Spam"
	Example: [PREFIX]mute @OmegaVesko 5 minutes "Trolling"
	You can leave out the time, in that case the mute will be indefinite.

Unmute a user:
    Usage: [PREFIX]unmute @user <"reason" (optional)>
	Example: [PREFIX]unmute @OmegaVesko

Ban a user:
    Usage: [PREFIX]ban @user <"reason" (optional)>
	Example: [PREFIX]ban @OmegaVesko

Kick a user:
    Usage: [PREFIX]kick  @user <"reason" (optional)>
	Example: [PREFIX]kick @OmegaVesko
```
