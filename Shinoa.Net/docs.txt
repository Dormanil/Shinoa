```
MAL (Anime):
    Usage: !anime <Anime Title>
	Fetches anime details from MyAnimeList.net.

MAL (Manga/LN):
	Usage: !manga <Manga/LN Title>
	Fetches manga or light novel details from MyAnimeList.net.

Anilist:
    Usage: !anilist <Anime Title>
	Fetches anime details from Anilist.co.

SAO Wikia:
	Usage: !sao <Query>
	Searches the SAO Wikia and returns the first result.

Wikipedia:
	Usage: !wiki <Query>
	Searches Wikipedia and returns the first result.
```
